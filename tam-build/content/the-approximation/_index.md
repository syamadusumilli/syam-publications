Can machines understand? Not in some distant future, but now. Not perfectly, but approximately. Not by achieving consciousness, but by approximating the functional patterns of human understanding well enough to matter.

These ten articles build the foundation: from functional understanding and intuition through irrationality and the limits of modeling, to the question of whether AI will ever feel, and what "good enough" means when it's your mind being approximated.
