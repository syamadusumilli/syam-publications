Something happens when you build systems that learn about people. The systems themselves become interesting. Not because they achieve consciousness, but because they develop behaviors, tendencies, and emergent properties worth studying on their own terms.

These articles treat AI not as a tool to evaluate but as a phenomenon to understand. What does it mean for an AI to be curious? What happens when AI agents form societies? What does negotiation look like when no humans are in the room?
