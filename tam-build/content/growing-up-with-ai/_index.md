No child was ever raised by one person. The village is not a backup system. It is the system. Now AI is entering that village: as companion, tutor, translator, scaffold.

These articles explore what this means for children, families, and development across the lifespan. From designing AI that grows children rather than simply comforts them, to the politics of multi-generational family systems, to the question of what bidirectional scaffolding looks like when one partner is a machine.
